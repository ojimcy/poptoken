How to buy

Create Wallet

Download trust wallet or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to trustwallet.com.

Acquire BNB tokens

Purchase BNB (Binance Coin) tokens or any token on the BSC network from a reputable exchange and transfer them to your wallet address. Skip if you already have in your wallet.

Visit PancakeSwap

On PancakeSwap, connect your wallet. Enter $POP Token Address: Once connected, click on the "Select a currency" field and choose "Search" or "Paste" to enter the $POP token address. Confirm that the token information is correct.

Swap BNB for $POP

Enter the amount of BNB you want to swap for $POP and click on the "Swap" button. Review the transaction details and confirm the swap. Once the transaction is confirmed, you will receive $POP tokens in your wallet.
